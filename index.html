Features

Everything you need for your WHV

📅

Visa Day Counter

Track your regional work days in real time. Know exactly where you stand toward your 88-day (2nd year) or 179-day (3rd year) requirement — no spreadsheets needed.

💰

Financial Dashboard

Log your gross pay per work day. Backtrack automatically calculates your superannuation and shows you exactly how much you'll recover via DASP before leaving Australia.

🗺️

Regional Employer Map

Find verified farms and employers in eligible postcode zones. Filter by region, job type and state. Know which employers count toward your visa before you move.

Built for both WHV paths

Whether you're going for your 2nd or 3rd year, Backtrack adapts your counter and targets automatically.
